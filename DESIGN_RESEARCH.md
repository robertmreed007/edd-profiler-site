# Fintech Compliance Website Design Research

## Common Patterns Across Jack Henry, Verafin, Abrigo, Alessa, NICE Actimize

### Color
- Deep navy/dark blue is the dominant base (every site uses dark hero sections)
- Teal/cyan is the most common accent for compliance-specific companies
- White for body content sections
- Green for "compliant/safe" signals, red for risk indicators

### Typography
- 100% sans-serif across all six companies (no serif fonts anywhere)
- Common: Inter, DM Sans, Be Vietnam Pro
- Large bold headlines (36-50px+), lighter body text
- Mixed-weight headlines trending (thin + bold in same line)

### Hero Section
- Dark backgrounds universal
- Left-aligned text + visual element on right is dominant
- Product mockup/illustration or abstract graphics (not stock photos)
- Structure: Badge → Headline → 1-2 sentences → 1-2 CTAs

### Features
- Tabbed interfaces or icon + text card grids (3-column)
- Statistics blocks with large bold numbers
- Case study cards with attribution

### Trust Signals
- Award badges (G2, Forrester, Celent, FinTech Breakthrough)
- Client logos in scrolling carousel
- Big-number statistics (transactions monitored, assets protected)
- Named testimonials with title and institution
- Certifications (SOC 1/2, ISO 27001, PCI)

### CTAs
- "Request Demo" / "Get a Demo" is the standard for compliance
- Pill-shaped buttons with rounded corners
- Persistent header CTA (always visible)
- Dual CTAs in hero (primary + secondary)

### The Formula
1. Dark navy/teal hero with large bold white text
2. Sans-serif typography only
3. "Get a Demo" CTA in pill button, always in sticky header
4. Statistics prominently displayed
5. Award badges / analyst recognition
6. Client logos carousel
7. White content sections alternating with dark/gray
8. Testimonials with named individuals
9. Footer with comprehensive sitemap

## Detailed Per-Company Analysis

### Jack Henry — Corporate Modern
- Royal blue primary, gold accent, video backgrounds
- Lowercase italic headlines (distinctive editorial feel)
- Tab-based feature presentation

### Verafin (Nasdaq) — Institutional Authority
- Deep navy-purple, teal accent, Earth-from-space imagery
- Massive data statistics ($11T assets, 1.8B transactions/week)
- Nasdaq brand association for credibility

### Abrigo — Community Bank Focused
- Dark teal primary, gold highlights, thin-weight typography
- 25+ award badges prominently displayed
- Community bank buyer persona (peer validation emphasis)

### Alessa — AML Specialist
- Near-black navy, teal accent, product illustrations
- Most focused AML/compliance positioning
- ROI Calculator as conversion tool (unique)
- Chatbot for immediate engagement

### NICE Actimize — Enterprise Leader
- Photography-heavy, rotating hero carousel
- "Know more. Risk less." tagline
- Heavy analyst recognition emphasis (Forrester, Celent)
